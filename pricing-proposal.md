# BNM Cafe Application - Pricing & Maintenance Proposal

## Overview
This document outlines the pricing models for the BNM Cafe application, detailing both a flexible monthly subscription model and a one-time final buyout option. It also covers post-launch maintenance and support.

---

## 1. Monthly Subscription Plan (SaaS Model)
This model allows for continuous use of the application with predictable monthly recurring costs. The company pays a single monthly invoice, taking care of both the infrastructure and software licensing.

**Suggested Monthly Fee:** ₹15,000 - ₹25,000 / month *(Feel free to adjust based on your desired profit margin)*

**Cost Breakdown:**
* **Production Overhead (Supabase, Vercel, Domain):** ₹5,000 / month 
* **Net Profit / Licensing Fee:** ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 / month

**Includes:**
* Full access to all platform features.
* Uptime monitoring and handling of standard infrastructure limits.
* Dedicated hosting management (no DevOps required from the company).

---

## 2. Final Buy-Out Option (Ownership Transfer)
This model transfers full ownership of the application, including the source code and intellectual property, directly to the company. 

**Suggested Buy-Out Price:** ₹2,50,000 - ₹4,00,000 *(One-time payment)*
*(Industry standard is calculating this as 12 to 24 months of the subscription value, plus the value of the custom source code IP).*

**Includes:**
* Full transfer of the GitHub repository and all source code.
* Complete handoff of Vercel production, Supabase database, and Domain registrar accounts (Billing is transferred directly to the company's credit card).
* Application documentation and setup guide.

**Note:** After a buy-out, the ₹5,000/month production costs will be paid directly by the company to the respective services (Vercel, Supabase, etc.).

---

## 3. Maintenance & Support Service (6 Months)
Following the initial deployment (or the buy-out), a 6-month maintenance contract is included to guarantee support for any unforeseen bugs or operational issues.

* **Duration:** 6 Months from the official launch/deployment date.
* **Pricing Model:** Hourly / On-Demand.
* **Suggested Hourly Rate:** ₹800 - ₹1,500 / hr *(Adjust based on your standard consulting/development rate)*

**Service Terms:**
* **Bug Fixes:** If any critical bugs or application errors appear, time spent investigating and resolving them will be tracked and billed on an hourly basis.
* **Feature Scope:** Any new feature additions, significant UI changes, or workflow modifications outside the originally agreed scope will also be billed at this hourly maintenance rate.
* **Response Time SLA:** Guaranteed acknowledgment within 24 hours of issue reporting.
